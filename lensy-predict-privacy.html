Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Lensy Predict ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Lensy Predict app.

1. Information We Collect

a. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal information automatically through third-party services, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • General location data (country or region only)

This data is collected via Google AdMob and Firebase services and is used solely to improve app performance and deliver relevant advertisements.

b. Personal Information

Lensy Predict does not require users to create accounts or submit personal information.

Administrator access uses Google Sign-In for authentication purposes only. This is limited to authorized administrators and is not available to general users.

2. Advertising

Lensy Predict displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

  • Ads help support free access to the app
  • Non-personalized ads may be shown depending on your consent and region
  • Rewarded ads may be offered to temporarily unlock premium content within the app

We comply fully with Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain user consent where required.

3. Consent Management

Upon first launch, users are shown an ads disclosure and consent request where applicable.

You can manage or update your consent choices at any time through the app’s settings.

4. Firebase Services

We use Firebase Firestore to store and retrieve app content such as match predictions.

Firebase may collect anonymized usage data in accordance with its own privacy policy.

Learn more: Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage

Some preferences (such as consent status) are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not store sensitive personal data locally.

6. Children’s Privacy

Lensy Predict is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase (Firestore, Authentication)
  • Google Sign-In (admin only)

Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
