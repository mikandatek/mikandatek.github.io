Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 21, 2025

Cover Letter Master is a one-time purchase, offline mobile application. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information Collection

Cover Letter Master does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (such as your name, job title, addresses, letter content, and signature) remains entirely on your device.

2. Offline Usage

The app works fully offline. No internet connection is required to generate, preview, edit, sign, export, or share cover letters.

3. Data Storage

Any files you save (such as PDF cover letters or signature images) are stored locally on your device and are fully under your control. The app does not access or manage your files beyond what is required to save or share them at your request.

4. Permissions

The app may request limited permissions solely to enable core functionality:

  • File access — to save PDF documents to your device
  • Temporary storage — to share PDFs using other apps

These permissions are used only when you explicitly perform related actions and are never used to collect or transmit data.

5. No Ads or Tracking

Cover Letter Master contains no advertisements, no analytics, and no tracking technologies. We do not use cookies, tracking SDKs, or third-party analytics services.

6. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any external servers or cloud services. PDF viewing, sharing, and file saving rely only on system-level services provided by your operating system.

7. Children’s Privacy

Cover Letter Master does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the official support channel listed on the app’s store page.
