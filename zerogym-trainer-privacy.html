Privacy Policy for ZeroGym Trainer

Last updated: January 07, 2026

ZeroGym Trainer is a fully offline bodyweight workout app designed to provide progressive calisthenics training without requiring any equipment, user accounts, or internet connection during usage.

1. Information We Collect

ZeroGym Trainer collects no personal information from users. The app does not:

  • Require sign-up, login, or user accounts.
  • Collect, store, or transmit personal data (such as name, email address, location, device ID, or fitness data).
  • Use analytics, tracking technologies, or advertising services.
  • Request or access any device permissions (including camera, microphone, storage, or location).

All exercises, images, and training logic are bundled within the app and stored locally on the user’s device.

2. Third-Party Services

ZeroGym Trainer uses the open-source Google Fonts package to display high-quality typography (Manrope and Inter fonts).

These fonts are bundled with the app at build time and do not require an internet connection to download or function during app usage.

ZeroGym Trainer does not integrate any other third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

3. Data Security

Since ZeroGym Trainer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of data exposure or breach originating from the app.

4. Children’s Privacy

ZeroGym Trainer does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13 and is compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). The app is safe to use for all age groups.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
