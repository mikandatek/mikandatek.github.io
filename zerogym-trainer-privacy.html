Privacy Policy for ZeroGym Trainer

Last updated: January 07, 2026

ZeroGym Trainer is a fully offline bodyweight workout app designed to provide progressive calisthenics training without requiring any equipment, accounts, or internet connection after initial setup.

1. Information We Collect

ZeroGym Trainer collects no personal information from users. The app does not:

  • Require sign-up or login.
  • Collect, store, or transmit any personal data (such as name, email, location, device ID, or fitness data).
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
  • Request any device permissions (e.g., camera, storage, location).

All app content (exercises, images, and training logic) is stored locally on your device and functions completely offline.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the open-source Google Fonts library to display premium typography (Manrope and Inter fonts). On first launch, the app downloads these fonts from Google’s servers and caches them locally for offline use thereafter.

Google may automatically collect standard connection information (such as IP address) during this one-time download, in accordance with Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy .

No other third-party services are used.

3. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data breach from the app.

4. Children’s Privacy

ZeroGym Trainer does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13 and is compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
